  • PROBLEM
  • The hospitality industry has little to no understanding how their employees’ and mangers’ interact with one another. What if there was an web/app based system that could help businesses understand the critical touch points users/clients identify with when a great experience is consumed? This can be a powerful tool that could shape the hospitality industry, but also reward those employees and staff who contribute to the experience through meaningful ways.
  • GOAL
  • Create a product geared towards the hosiptality industry that reshapes the thinking and interaction between the managers, employees and clients.
USER RESEARCH

Research, Personas, User Flows & Final Designs

RESEARCH FINDINGS

The research phase began with surveying and a day-in-the-life analysis of 50 users, both managers and contributors: cooks, bartenders, servers and dishwashers. The users were analyzed to get a deep dive into their position, how they interact with one another, expectations, pain points, etc., in order to drive empathy and next steps in the process.

With the data documented and on top of mind, I was able to establish a clear understanding on how to move forward, which will later translate into personas. The findings and takeaways from the survey and day-in-the-life are as follows:

  • TAKEAWAYS & CONSIDERATIONS
  • Managers want to know who their best workers are to put best foot forward.
    I want to be able to voice my concerns
    Understand the contributors (heroes) needs.
    Be able to communicate, self-organize, and update my status.
    How do I move up and make more money.
    Things change, how do I keep everyone up to speed?
    How do I know the clients had an enjoyable experience?
    I need to rate my employees.
